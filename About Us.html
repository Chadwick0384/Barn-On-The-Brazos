Hello,

We are Cathy and Curtis Haley, and welcome to our wedding venue!

Based on 5-star ratings and reviews from our clients we were chosen to be one of The Knots "Best Of" Wedding Venues for 2019 in the state of Texas! The Barn on the Brazos is the perfect venue for you if you want a true country style wedding. The Barn is a rustic yet elegant venue to host your wedding or special event. Enjoy an outdoor ceremony at the arbors during sunset that overlook the beautiful countryside. Alongside the barn are silos that light up the night sky. The Barn on the Brazos sits on an 880 acre working cattle ranch run by Curtis. The line of the land has amazing views from bluffs overlooking the Brazos River that make for great photo opportunities.

We give you the power to customize every aspect of your event, from the decor and floral arrangements to the menue and entertainment. We'll make sure the special day you've envisioned becomes a reality. Contact us to book your date today. We can't wait to host you! We are located south of the Dallas/Forth Worth area.
